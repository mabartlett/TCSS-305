Welcome, Fellow Student

What you've discovered is the product of Marcus's note-taking for the Winter quarter of TCSS 305 with Professor Athirai Irissappane. Yes, that ceaseless keyboard-slapping during class is actually being put to good use. In the spirit of mutual aid, I've decided that everybody would benefit from taking a peak at my notes. A good portion of it is copied from the lecture slides and the rest of it is sloppy as all hell, but hey, it's free!

Click one of the links in the navigation bar along the left to start studying.

This website, like the school quarter that produced it, is still unfinished. in addition to adding more notes, I will be implementing a more appropriate design for mobile viewports and maybe throw in some sexy Google fonts or something. I also feel like this boring landing page needs something more, like maybe some stock photos of 20-something-year-olds drinking coffee. People love those! Stay tuned and check in every week to see what's new. For those who are curious, this site was made with the static site generator Jekyll and all the notes were taken in the Markdown format.

Good luck on your exams and may the Force be with you.
